In Brief

Stephanie Gravenor is a highly analytic industrial engineer who is focused on the use of information to advance strategic and operational goals of major academic medical centers. Stephanie currently works as the Clinical Analytics Lead for the Department of Emergency Medicine at Northwestern Memorial HealthCare. She is also pursuing her MBA at Northwestern University's Kellogg School of Management with a concentration on healthcare and entrepreneurship.
